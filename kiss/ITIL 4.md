## **2.2 The ITIL Service Value System (SVS)**

The SVS is the core of ITIL 4. It explains how an organization transforms demand into value.

The SVS includes:

* **Guiding Principles**
* **Governance**
* **Service Value Chain**
* **Practices**
* **Continuous Improvement**

---

## **2.3 The Seven Guiding Principles**

These are universal rules for decision-making:

1. **Focus on value**
2. **Start where you are**
3. **Progress iteratively with feedback**
4. **Collaborate and promote visibility**
5. **Think and work holistically**
6. **Keep it simple and practical**
7. **Optimize and automate**

These principles are ESSENTIAL for applying ITIL effectively.
